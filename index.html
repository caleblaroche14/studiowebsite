About Me

Hey, I’m Caleb — the producer and mixer behind Sunset Run Studio in Franklin County, VT. With over 12 years of experience, I’ve produced EDM albums as Kid Zion, metal with DoomWarden, and synthwave/indie tracks on Terminate and Stay Resident. I specialize in mixing, mastering, songwriting, and helping artists bring their ideas to life — no experience required.
